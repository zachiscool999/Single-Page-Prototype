Pong is widely recognized as the first video game to achieve commercial success, becoming a cornerstone of the video game industry. It was developed by the Atari co-founder, Allan Alcorn. Pong was released in 1972 but orginally was not inteded to be a major product. It was actually just a simple training excersize for Alcorn to help him learn the basics of game design.
